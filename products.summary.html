Product Summary

Netflix's core business centers on digital media streaming and original content production, targeting general consumers with subscription-based entertainment and diversifying through an ad-supported tier and mobile games to expand its content and revenue models.

Conversely, Nvidia's core business is in visual computing, providing hardware and software primarily for gaming and AI, and it strategically diversifies by applying its GPU technology across high-growth, business-to-business markets such as data center AI solutions, professional visualization, and specialized automotive chips, broadening its reach significantly beyond its foundational consumer gaming market.
