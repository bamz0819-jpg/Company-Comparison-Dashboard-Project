Products & Services Breakdown

A side-by-side comparison of what each company offers.

Netflix, Inc.

  • Streaming Subscriptions: Offers various tiers (Basic, Standard, Premium) with different features like quality and number of concurrent streams.
  • Original Content Production: Creates and licenses its own movies, TV shows, documentaries, and specials.
  • Ad-Supported Tier: Provides a lower-cost option with advertisements.
  • Gaming: Expanded into mobile gaming, offering a selection of games to subscribers at no extra cost.

NVIDIA Corporation

  • Gaming GPUs: Designs GeForce graphics processing units for PC gaming.
  • Data Center: Provides GPUs and software for AI, machine learning, and high-performance computing.
  • Professional Visualization: Creates Quadro and RTX graphics cards for professional workstations (e.g., film and design).
  • Automotive: Develops chips and software for self-driving cars and AI-powered cockpits.
