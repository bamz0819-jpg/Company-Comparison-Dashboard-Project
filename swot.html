SWOT Analysis

A strategic overview of strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.

Netflix SWOT

Strengths

  • Market leadership and strong brand recognition
  • Massive content library, including original productions

Weaknesses

  • High content production costs
  • Dependence on ad-supported models for new revenue

Opportunities

  • Expansion into gaming and interactive content
  • Growing demand in emerging international markets

Threats

  • Intense competition from rival streamers (Disney+, Max)
  • Piracy and account sharing

Nvidia SWOT

Strengths

  • Dominant market share in the AI chip sector
  • Strong ecosystem with CUDA software platform

Weaknesses

  • Dependence on a few large customers
  • High prices for consumer GPUs

Opportunities

  • Growth in automotive and robotics markets
  • Cloud computing and enterprise AI solutions

Threats

  • Rapid innovation from competitors (AMD, Intel)
  • Supply chain disruptions and geopolitical risks
